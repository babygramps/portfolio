How to play
  • Each player places pieces of their color, starting from their corner of the board.
  • Every new piece must touch one of your own pieces at a corner — but may never share an edge with your own color.
  • Touching other players' pieces is allowed anywhere.
  • Click a piece in the tray, rotate (R) or flip (F) it, then click the board. On touch screens, tap the board to preview, then hit ✓ Place.
  • When you can't move, you're done. Fewest squares left over wins — place all 21 pieces for a +15 bonus (+20 if the single square is last).
